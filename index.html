"Seventeen years of teaching, research, and leadership — building antennas, networks, and a department worth being proud of."

Dr. P. Rajeswari currently serves as Associate Professor & Head of the Department of ECE at Dhanalakshmi Srinivasan Engineering College (Autonomous), Perambalur — a role she has held since 2022. She completed her Ph.D. in ECE, specialising in Mobile Application Development, at Anna University, Chennai, earning a "Highly Commended" distinction in 2022.

Her research spans microstrip antenna analysis, wireless sensor networks, and mobile application development, with 26 journal publications — including SCI-indexed work in Microprocessors and Microsystems (Elsevier) — and over 60 conference papers presented at IEEE and international venues. She holds two filed patents and has guided 15 postgraduate and 28 undergraduate project batches.

Beyond the classroom, she has led three separate accreditation efforts to completion — NAAC "A" Grade, NBA (Tier I & II), and ten-year Autonomous status — and established both a Communication Systems Lab and a Centre of Excellence in IoT.
